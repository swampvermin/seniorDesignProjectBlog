week 10 [ mar. 25, 24 ]

i made some test prints this week in order to test out different color combinations. i think my final choices for zine color are:
1. [zine 1: washington sq. park] blue + yellow
2. [zine 2: collect pond] blue + orange
3. [zine 3: brooklyn heights promenade]: blue + pink




i like keeping the blue theme, after doing some more test prints ive learned its best to keep the amount of blue ink to a minimum in order to not muddy the details. i also realized i need to keep images large in order to be able to see details.
